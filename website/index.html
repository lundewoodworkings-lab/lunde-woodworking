About Me

I’m a young woodworker from Minnesota building a woodworking business from the ground up. I focus on clean design, tight tolerances, and making pieces that last. From small projects to custom furniture, I approach every build with care and intention. I started woodworking in 2022 as a fun COVID hobby, but I quickly realized how much I enjoyed it and knew from that moment on that it’s what I wanted to do with my life. I originally worked out of a corner of my parents’ garage, where I learned the fundamentals of the craft. Over time, that grew into renovating a shed into a small woodworking shop, which is where I work to this day. Hopefully, one day I’ll be able to upgrade to a larger, dedicated shop.
